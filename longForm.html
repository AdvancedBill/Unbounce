Still trying to decide if you even need hearing aids?

In our experience, as we get older, most of us only notice our hearing loss at certain times or places, or under certain circumstances. Such as…

  • In restaurants. Holding a conversation in a restaurant is one of the most common complaints we here from anyone with hearing loss.

  • In large crowds. Big crowds bring a lot of background noise making it practically impossible to hear a single voice.

  • On the phone. Telephone conversations can be very difficult for all levels of hearing loss.

  • Watching TV. Everyone with hearing loss wants to turn the volume up… and up… and up – and this can be annoying for everyone else.
